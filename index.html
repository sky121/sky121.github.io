A Little About Myself

My name is Skylar Hoffman and I am attending UC Irvine and majoring in Computer Science. I love creating and developing unique ideas and solving complex problems. As a programmmer I have always excelled in persistantly tackling bugs and errors without hesistating or giving up.

I love the feeling of solving a difficult, challenging issue with a program and viewing the completion and full compilation of its code. I never am just focused on completing a programming assignment, I push myself to try and solve it in the most effiecient best way possible. In my freetime, I love to do many activities such as creating and listening to music. Over the last two summers I have taught myself how to play guitar and have found that by letting out my creative nature into music, I am able to fully comprehend and allow my feelings and thoughts to flow.

Music has always brought out the deepest of emotions in me and by playing guitar I am able to truly release my stresses. Another love of mine is bowling. I spent a good part of my time becoming a better bowler with my friends because I love the challenge. The feeling of compressing all ones strength into a powerful release of a bowling ball is quite an amazing experience. Additionially, I love animals, with a passion for dogs, archery, hiking, and nature. Since my childhood, my parents have brought me to beautiful, serene hiking trails where the smell of freshly sprouting trees and the dew on vastly growing forests have always inspired me.
