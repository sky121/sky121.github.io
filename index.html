Lyffe Style

Lyffe Style is a Chrome extension that takes the style of one image and transplants it onto any image you mouse over while browsing Google. For example, you can input the URL of Vincent Van Gogh's The Starry Night and move your mouse over the Google Image search results of "dog," and see pictures of dogs that look like they were painted with that iconic blue swirl look.

Syrup

Syrup is a chrome extension designed for Ebay allowing you create a bidding queue of similar listings of an item you want. With this bidding queue, Syrup automatically finds the lowest priced item in the list and bids for you (not exceeding your maximum of course). When you've been outbid, Syrup goes through the list for the best possible deal and bids on that item instead. Syrup was built for HackTech, a coding competition hosted by CalTech University. Our project won for best use of MongoDB, a nonSQL database program hosted on the cloud.
