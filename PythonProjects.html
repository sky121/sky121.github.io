Python Studies

Two interactive works in Pygame and Tkinter. Student work, 2018–2021.

The two principal pieces from my time learning Python. The first is Columns, a game in the spirit of Tetris, with Pygame supplying the physics and the graphical surface. The second, made with Tkinter, is a ball simulator that uses object inheritance to drive an interactive, changing scene.
