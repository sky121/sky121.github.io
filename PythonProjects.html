Python Studies

Two playful works in motion — falling gems and drifting spheres, painted in Pygame and Tkinter.

The two principal pieces from my time learning Python. The first is Columns, a game in the spirit of Tetris, with Pygame supplying the physics and the graphical surface. The second, made with Tkinter, is a ball simulator that uses a small menagerie of objects and inheritance to create an interactive, ever-changing scene.
